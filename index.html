Getting Around Singapore

Navigate Singapore's world-class transport system with ease

MRT (Mass Rapid Transit)

Singapore's subway system is fast, efficient, and air-conditioned. Perfect for getting around!

  • Get an EZ-Link card or use SimplyGo with contactless payment
  • Download the MyTransport.SG app for real-time updates
  • Off-peak hours (before 7:45am): Save up to 25%
  • Nearest to RP: Woodlands MRT Station
üöå

Buses

Extensive bus network connecting every corner of Singapore

  • Use Google Maps or MyTransport.SG for bus routes
  • Buses to RP: 856, 900, 903, 911, 913
  • Always tap in AND out with your card
  • Bus stops have digital displays showing arrival times
üöñ

Grab & Gojek

Ride-hailing apps for convenient door-to-door travel

  • Download Grab or Gojek apps
  • Split fares with friends using GrabShare
  • Compare prices before booking
  • Great for late nights or heavy shopping

üí° Pro Tip

Get a concession card if you're under 25! Student fares can save you significant money on transport.

Affordable Food Options

Eat well without breaking the bank - Singapore's food scene has it all

üçú

Hawker Centres

The heart of Singapore's food culture - delicious and budget-friendly

  • Meals from $3-$6 SGD
  • Huge variety: Chinese, Malay, Indian, Western
  • Near RP: Woodlands 888 Plaza, Marsiling Mall
  • Don't forget to return your tray!
$3-6 per meal
üç±

Food Courts

Air-conditioned comfort with diverse food choices

  • Slightly pricier than hawkers: $5-$8 SGD
  • Found in malls and commercial buildings
  • RP Campus: Multiple food options on-site
  • Great for study sessions with friends
$5-8 per meal
üõí

Supermarkets & Self-Cooking

Save even more by preparing your own meals

  • FairPrice, Sheng Siong, Cold Storage
  • Weekly groceries: $30-$50 SGD
  • Markets for fresh produce at lower prices
  • Perfect if your accommodation has a kitchen
$30-50 per week

Chicken Rice, Laksa, Char Kway Teow, Roti Prata, Nasi Lemak, Satay - all affordable and delicious!
